Chenhao Zhang, Ph.D., Master’s Supervisor, is a faculty member in the School of Computer Science, China University of Petroleum (East China). He received his Ph.D. in Engineering from Beihang University in 2025, majoring in Computer Architecture. His current research interests include computer storage systems, high-performance computing, reinforcement learning, and edge computing. He has published multiple papers in top international conferences and journals in computer science, including CCF-A venues such as ICDE, AAAI, ISCA, and IEEE TC. He has led five national and provincial-level projects, including a subtask of a major project of the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology and the Shandong Provincial Youth Fund. His research results have been applied in national supercomputing environments, China Mobile, and the Second Academy of China Aerospace, and were selected for the National 13th Five-Year Plan Science and Technology Innovation Achievement Exhibition. He also serves as a reviewer for several CCF-recommended conferences and journals, including ICML, NeurIPS, and TJSC.
